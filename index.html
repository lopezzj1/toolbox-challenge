Let's Play a Memory Game!

How to Play:

There are a total of 16 tiles. The goal of this game is to match 8 of the tiles to its other pair. If you click one tile, it will turn face up and reveal an image. You can only click two tiles at a time. If the two tiles' images match, they will stay on it's image side. If the two tiles do not match, it will flip back to its orignal state.
